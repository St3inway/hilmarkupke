CV - Curriculum Vitae

As at: Spring 2024

  • Hilmar Kupke studied violin and viola in Dresden, Berlin and London
  • He was a member of the Ensemble Oriol/Kammerakademie Potsdam and the Zeitgenössische Oper Berlin, played in baroque ensembles such as the Florilegium Ensemble London, Ensemble Aris and Aulis Berlin and La Visione Kassel.
  • Invitations have taken him to the Kassel State Theatre and the Nuremberg State Opera as a solo violist.
  • He is a frequent guest with the Staatskapelle Dresden.
  • From 2001 - 2024 he was principal violist of the Regensburg Philharmonic Orchestra.
  • For many years, he played sonata recitals with his wife and duo partner Viola Kupke until her death in 2013, in which they contrasted contemporary works with those of the tonal repertoire. Since then, he has continued this work with changing chamber music partners.

Teaching

Music is part of my life, like breathing. It expresses all facets of my existence.
I want to make this tangible for other people too.

I want to explore the questions that arise at any given moment through the eyes of my counterpart.
These may be questions about mental, physical, musical or technical challenges - and the connections between these fields.
Children, young people or adults, amateurs or professionals, everyone is welcome!

  • Beginner
  • Advanced
  • Preparation for entrance exams
  • Audition preparations
  • Orchestral studies
  • Concert preparations
  • Coaching from professionals (‘lifelong learning’)

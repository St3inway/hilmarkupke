Welcome

My name is Hilmar Kupke, I am a freelance violinist and violist. Previously I was employed as a solo violist at Regensburg Theatre for 22 years and decided to return to freelance music in 2024. I love being on stage (or sitting in the pit) with exciting musicians in changing formations, from chamber music to chamber orchestra to symphony orchestra! I have performed with the Kammerakademie Potsdam, the baroque ensembles Aris&Aulis Berlin, Concerto Brandenburg and La Visione Kassel. I am also a regular guest with the Staatskapelle Dresden. As a solo violist, I have played projects at the Kassel State Theatre, the Nuremberg State Opera, the Staatskapelle Weimar and as deputy principal violist at the Komische Oper Berlin. I also combine violin and viola in my sonata recitals. For my programmes, I have already arranged a number of works from the violin and cello literature for the viola, such as the cello sonatas by Rachmaninov and Chopin or the D minor violin sonata by Schumann. I have also expanded the concerto repertoire and transferred Schumann's Violin Concerto to the viola. This work is sometimes criticised for the fact that the solo violin is too often played in the middle register. In my viola arrangement, the solo instrument shows its full range and radiance! Schumann made an arrangement for violin of his Concerto for Cello and Orchestra. - so it was only natural for me to make an version for viola here too. I performed both works with the Regensburg Philharmonic Orchestra.
My heart beats especially for historical performance practice. It helps me to find new colours and come into contact with myself and my colleagues in a more lively way. I am just as committed to the music of our time - where our present becomes sound and I make contemporary voices heard through my work.
I consider myself lucky to be able to follow this freelance path as a musician!

CV - Curriculum Vitae

As at: Spring 2024

  • Hilmar Kupke is a violinist and violist.
  • He is particularly focused on historical performance practice and the diverse music of the present day.
  • He is a guest of the Kammerakademie Potsdam, the baroque ensembles Aris&Aulis Berlin, Concerto Brandenburg and La Visione Kassel.
  • He frequently receives invitations to perform with the Staatskapelle Dresden.
  • From 2002 to 2024 he was principal violist of the Regensburg Philharmonic Orchestra, with whom he regularly performed as a soloist.
  • Invitations have taken him as principal viola to the Staatstheater Kassel, the Nuremberg State Opera, the Staatskapelle Weimar and as deputy principal viola at the Komische Oper Berlin.
  • He has held positions in the Lübeck Philharmonic Orchestra (section leader of the 2nd violins) and the Stuttgart Philharmonic Orchestra.
  • He was a member of the Ensemble Oriol/Kammerakademie Potsdam and the Zeitgenössische Oper Berlin, as well as a guest of the Baroque Ensemble Florilegium UK and the European Community Chamber Orchestra.
  • Hilmar Kupke studied violin and viola in Dresden, Berlin and London with Ilan Gronich, Hartmut Rohde and David Takeno, among others.
  • For many years, until her death in 2010, he played sonata recitals with his wife and duo partner Viola Kupke, in which they contrasted contemporary works with those of the tonal repertoire in exciting programmes. Since then he has continued this work with changing chamber music partners.

Teaching

Music is part of my life, as much as breathing. It expresses all facets of my existence.
This experience I try to make accessible to other people. I want to explore the questions that arise at any given moment through the eyes of my counterpart.

These may be questions about mental, physical, musical or technical challenges - and the connections between these fields. Children, young people or adults, amateurs or professionals, everyone is welcome!

  • Get started
  • Advanced
  • Preparation for entrance exams
  • Audition preparations
  • Orchestral studies
  • Concert preparations
  • Coaching of professionals (‘lifelong learning’)

Welcome

I am pleased...

... that you are interested in browsing my website! I am a violinist and violist and have been combining both instruments in my sonata recitals for many years. My desire to expand the viola literature has also led to a number of arrangements of violin and cello literature. This enriches both my sonata programmes and the list of viola concertos. For example, I played my arrangements of Robert Schumann's Violin Concerto and Cello Concerto with the Regensburg Philharmonic Orchestra with great success. For many years, I explored these fields alongside my orchestral festival position as principal violist in Regensburg. My desire to have more creative freedom for chamber music and solo projects led to my decision to leave this position in 2024 and work as a freelance musician. For many years, I have focussed on historical performance practice and exploring the diverse music of our time. I find the mixture of freelance projects and engagements in state orchestras very enriching.

CV - Curriculum Vitae

As at: Spring 2024

  • Hilmar Kupke is a violinist and violist.
  • He is particularly focussed on historical performance practice and the diverse music of the present day.
  • He is a guest of the Kammerakademie Potsdam, the baroque ensembles Aris&Aulis Berlin, Concerto Brandenburg and La Visione Kassel.
  • He frequently receives invitations to perform with the Staatskapelle Dresden.
  • From 2002 to 2024 he was principal violist of the Regensburg Philharmonic Orchestra, with whom he regularly performed as a soloist.
  • Invitations have taken him to the Staatstheater Kassel, the Nuremberg State Opera, the Staatskapelle Weimar and as deputy principal viola at the Komische Opera Berlin.
  • He has held positions in the Lübeck Philharmonic Orchestra (section leader of the 2nd violins) and the Stuttgart Philharmonic Orchestra.
  • He was a member of the Ensemble Oriol/Kammerakademie Potsdam and the Zeitgenössische Oper Berlin, as well as a guest of the Baroque Ensemble Florilegium UK and the European Community Chamber Orchestra.
  • Hilmar Kupke studied violin and viola in Dresden, Berlin and London with Ilan Gronich, Hartmut Rohde and David Takeno, among others.
  • For many years, until her death in 2010, he played sonata recitals with his wife and duo partner Viola Kupke, in which they contrasted contemporary works with those of the tonal repertoire in exciting programmes. Since then he has continued this work with changing chamber music partners.

Teaching

Music is part of my life, as much as breathing. It expresses all facets of my existence.
I want to make this experience accessible to other people. I want to explore the questions that arise at any given moment through the eyes of my counterpart.

These may be questions about mental, physical, musical or technical challenges - and the connections between these fields. Children, young people or adults, amateurs or professionals, everyone is welcome!

  • Get started
  • Advanced
  • Preparation for entrance exams
  • Audition preparations
  • Orchestral studies
  • Concert preparations
  • Coaching of professionals (‘lifelong learning’)
